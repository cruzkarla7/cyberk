# Non-Disclosure Agreements and Intellectual Property Protection Policy

## NDA Execution Requirements
- All employees, contractors, and third-parties must sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before accessing sensitive information.
- The NDA must be renewed annually or as needed when roles and responsibilities change.

## Information Handling Procedures
- Confidential information must be labeled and stored in secured environments.
- Access to sensitive data should be restricted to authorized personnel only.
- Any data sharing must be conducted through secured channels.

## Intellectual Property Protection Measures
- All intellectual property created within the course of employment will belong to the organization.
- Employees must disclose any invention or idea developed using company resources to the management.

## Violation Response Procedures
- Any violation of NDA or intellectual property policies will be investigated promptly.
- Disciplinary actions may include termination, legal action, and financial penalties.

## Monitoring and Enforcement Mechanisms
- Regular audits will be conducted to ensure compliance with NDA and IP policies.
- Employees will be trained on the importance of NDAs and how to protect intellectual property.

## Training Requirements
- All employees must undergo training on NDA, privacy, and intellectual property policies during onboarding.
- Refresher courses will be held annually.

## Effective Date
- This policy is effective as of April 1, 2026.
