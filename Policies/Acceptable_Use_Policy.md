# Acceptable Use Policy

## Introduction
This Acceptable Use Policy outlines the acceptable use of our services, including online activities and communication. 

## User Responsibilities
1. Users must comply with all applicable laws and regulations.
2. Users must respect the rights of others and refrain from engaging in harmful activities.

## Prohibited Activities
1. Harassment, threats, or abuse of other users.
2. Distribution of malicious software or viruses.
3. Unauthorized access to systems or accounts.

## Consequences of Violation
Violations of this policy may result in suspension or termination of services.

## Policy Changes
We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time, with changes taking effect immediately upon posting.
